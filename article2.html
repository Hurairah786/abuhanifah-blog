Advice on Seeking Knowledge

Imam Abu Hanifah (رح)'s Advice to his Student Imam Abu Yusuf (رح)

translations biography 3 March 2026 · 3 min read

A Translation of Imam Abu Hanifah's advice to his student Imam Abu Yusuf

"Seek knowledge first, then gather wealth through permissible means, and then get married.

For if you engage in the pursuit of wealth whilst seeking knowledge, you will be deficient in seeking knowledge.

The wealth will encourage you to buy (things)… and become preoccupied with worldly affairs.

And beware of becoming distracted by women before acquiring knowledge.

Your time is lost and your offspring will increase. As your family increases, you will be burdened with fulfilling their needs, and you will abandon knowledge.

And preoccupy yourself with seeking knowledge from the prime of your youth, during the free time of your heart and mind.

Then engage yourself in the pursuit of wealth so that you may earn.

Certainly, a lot of children and family will confuse the mind. Thereafter, if you've gathered wealth, then seek to get married."
